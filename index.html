About us

Tech Diaspora is a special platform that gathers Azerbaijanis in tech fields from all around the world. We suggest regular meetups of members, community events, mentoring support and investment opportunities.

Our Services

Community Building

We connect our community members to enable a better flow of capital, talent, and ideas by organizing networking events and providing community networking tools. Magic happens when you meet new, interesting people.

Adviser & Mentorship

We provide mentoring services to startups and businesses in the field of technology, helping them become professional and globalized. We also provide services for the application of innovative solutions in the field of technology. Our mentor and adviser helps our members on this path.

Investment & Co-investment

We Inspire creation of next generation communities of tech business champions. We spark new ideas, explain the fast-changing world around us and the impact the new technologies may have on businesses and society in future.

Co-foundering & Matching

We mediate the construction of new large projects by bringing together co-founders. We always support the co-founders in great activities together.

What we do

Our vision

We connect leaders and future leaders of this industry who identify themselves as Azerbaijani and share common values, regardless of their current place of living, national identity or ethnicity: transmigrants, expats, digital nomads or simply - global minds.

News

Our local startup Whelp was admitted to the Canadian Techstars Acceleration Program.

According to the terms, the startup, headed by Seymur Rasulov, also attracted investment from the program. Note that our partner SUP.VC the event was co-organized by Azerbaijan's Ministry of culture and tourism Congratulations to Seymour and his team!

International digital payment system “Paypal " starts its activity in Azerbaijan.

The head of the Azexport portal, the operator of the Center for analysis of economic reforms and Communications (IITGM), Aykhan Gadashov, said at the signing ceremony of the memorandum of cooperation with Azerpost LLC of the Ministry of Transport, Communications and high technologies. ” At present, PayPal is partially operating in our country. Certain work must be done to ensure its full functioning. In particular, some legislative acts need to be amended, and of course, Azerpost also plays a big role in this, “he said.

Google opens third Office of new brand in Baku

Technology giant Google continues to innovate. Thus, the company, which entered the game industry, created a new brand called" Google Developers Group Games " (GDG). Google has opened the third center of Google Developers Group Games in Baku. The center opened in Baku was opened in partnership with local gaming company Gamepons.
